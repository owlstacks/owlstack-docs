## Free tier

The free tier is perfect for trying OwlStack:

- 1 platform connection
- 10 posts per month
- Basic SDK features (Post builder, Media, DeliveryResult)
- Community support

No credit card required.

## Annual discount

Save up to 25% with annual billing:

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Savings |
|:-----|:--------|:-------|:--------|

## Getting started

1. [Sign up](https://app.owlstack.dev) for a free account
2. Choose a plan (or start free)
3. Get your API key
4. [Install the SDK](/getting-started/installation) and start publishing
