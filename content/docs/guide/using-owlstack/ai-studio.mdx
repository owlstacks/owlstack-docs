# AI Studio

OwlStack's AI Studio helps you create better social media content faster. Available on all plans.

## What AI Studio Can Do

### Generate Content

Don't know what to post? Describe a topic and AI Studio will generate platform-optimized posts for you.

**Example:**
- You type: "We just launched our new coffee blend"
- AI generates posts tailored for Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc. — each with the right tone, length, and hashtags

### Optimize Existing Content

Paste in your draft and AI Studio will suggest improvements:

- Better wording for engagement
- Optimal length for each platform
- Stronger call-to-action
- Better hashtag suggestions

### Auto-Generate Captions

Upload an image or video, and AI Studio will generate a caption based on the visual content.

### Smart Hashtags

AI suggests relevant hashtags based on:

- Your content
- Current trends
- Your audience
- Platform best practices

## How to Use AI Studio

1. Click **AI Studio** in the sidebar, or click the **AI** icon when creating a post
2. Choose what you want to do: **Generate**, **Optimize**, or **Auto-Caption**
3. Provide your input (topic, draft, or media)
4. Review the AI suggestions
5. Edit if needed, then use in your post

## AI Credits

AI features use credits from your monthly allowance:

| Plan | AI Credits / Month |
|:-----|:------------------|
| **Starter** | 200 |
| **Pro** | 1,000 |
| **Pro Plus** | 2,000 |

Each generation, optimization, or auto-caption uses 1 credit. Credits reset monthly.
