# Connecting Your Social Accounts

OwlStack supports publishing to 11+ social media platforms. Here's how to connect each one.

## How to Connect a Platform

1. Log in to [app.owlstack.dev](https://app.owlstack.dev)
2. Go to **Settings → Social Accounts**
3. Click **Connect** next to the platform you want
4. You'll be redirected to the platform to authorize OwlStack
5. Once authorized, your account appears as **Connected**

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | What You Need |
|:---------|:-------------|
| **Instagram** | An Instagram Business or Creator account |
| **Twitter / X** | A Twitter account |
| **Facebook** | A Facebook Page you admin |
| **LinkedIn** | A LinkedIn profile or Company Page |
| **Telegram** | A Telegram channel or group where you're admin |
| **Discord** | A Discord server where you have admin permissions |
| **Pinterest** | A Pinterest Business account |
| **Reddit** | A Reddit account |
| **Slack** | A Slack workspace where you're admin |
| **Tumblr** | A Tumblr blog |
| **WhatsApp** | A WhatsApp Business account |

## How Many Accounts Can I Connect?

## Troubleshooting

### "Authorization failed"

- Make sure you're logged into the correct account on the platform
- Check that you have admin permissions (for Pages, Channels, etc.)
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the account

### "Account disconnected"

Some platforms require re-authorization periodically. Go to **Social Accounts** and click **Reconnect** next to the affected platform.

### Platform-Specific Notes

- **Instagram**: Requires a Facebook Business account linked to your Instagram
- **Telegram**: You'll need a Bot Token from [BotFather](https://t.me/botfather) and the bot must be admin in your channel
- **WhatsApp**: Requires a WhatsApp Business API account
