# Plans Overview

OwlStack offers three plans to match your needs — from solo creators to growing teams.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Starter | Pro | Pro Plus |
|:--------|:--------|:----|:---------|
| **Monthly price** | $19/mo | $49/mo | $99/mo |
| **Yearly price** | $15/mo | $39/mo | $49/mo |
| Social accounts | 10 | 10 | 30 |
| AI credits | 200/mo | 1,000/mo | 2,000/mo |
| Media library | 10 GB | 50 GB | Unlimited |
| Workspaces | 1 | 1 | 3 |
| Analytics | Basic | Advanced | Advanced |
| API access | Basic | Basic | Advanced |

## Included in All Plans

Every OwlStack plan includes:

- **Unlimited scheduled posts & drafts**
- **Unlimited team members**
- First comment scheduling
- AI Content Studio
- Auto caption generator
- Content calendar
- Viral video library
- Bulk scheduling
- Priority support

## Choosing a Plan

- **Starter** ($19/mo): Great for solo creators, freelancers, and small businesses getting started with social media management.
- **Pro** ($49/mo): For businesses that need advanced analytics and more AI-powered content generation.
- **Pro Plus** ($99/mo): For teams and agencies managing multiple brands with up to 30 social accounts and 3 workspaces.
