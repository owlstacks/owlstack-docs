# Why OwlStack?

There are plenty of social media management tools out there. Here's why OwlStack is different.

---

## Built for Developers & Teams

Most social media tools are built for marketers. OwlStack is built for **developers and teams** who need both a powerful dashboard and a real API.

- **Full REST API** on every plan — not locked behind enterprise pricing
- **Official SDKs** for PHP, Laravel, and WordPress — with Node.js, Python, Go, Ruby, and Rust coming soon
- **Webhooks** for real-time event notifications
- **Self-hostable** — deploy on your own infrastructure if you need full control

---

## One API, 11+ Platforms

Write one integration. Publish everywhere.

Instead of building and maintaining separate integrations for Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Telegram, Discord, and more — OwlStack gives you a single, consistent API that handles all of them.

| What OwlStack handles for you |
|:------------------------------|
| Platform-specific formatting and character limits |
| Media processing and optimization |
| Rate limiting and queue management |
| Retry logic with exponential backoff |
| Thread splitting (Twitter), carousels (Instagram), embeds (Discord) |

---

## Unlimited Team Members

Every OwlStack plan includes **unlimited team members** — no per-seat pricing. Invite your entire team without worrying about costs scaling with headcount.

---

## AI-Powered Content

AI is built into every plan, not sold as an add-on:

- **Generate captions** — AI writes engaging post copy for any platform
- **Optimize content** — Get suggestions to improve reach and engagement
- **Hashtag recommendations** — AI-powered hashtag suggestions based on your content
- **Multi-platform adaptation** — AI adjusts tone and format for each platform

---

## Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees. No per-seat charges. No surprise enterprise pricing.

- Every plan includes API access
- Every plan includes unlimited team members
- Every plan includes AI features
- Upgrade or downgrade anytime

---

## Open Ecosystem

OwlStack isn't a walled garden:

- **Open-source SDKs** — all SDKs are open-source on GitHub
- **WordPress plugin** — official plugin for WordPress publishers
- **Laravel package** — first-class Laravel integration
- **Webhooks** — integrate with any system
- **REST API** — build anything you need

---

## Self-Hosting Option

Need full control over your data? OwlStack can be deployed on your own servers. Run the entire platform on your infrastructure with Docker.
