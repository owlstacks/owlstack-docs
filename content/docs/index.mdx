# Welcome to OwlStack

**OwlStack** is a social media management platform that lets you publish, schedule, and manage content across 11+ social media platforms — all from one place.

Whether you're a content creator, a business owner, or a developer building an integration, OwlStack has you covered.

---

## For Creators & Teams

No coding required. Use the OwlStack dashboard to manage your social media presence.

- **Publish to all your platforms at once** — write once, share everywhere
- **Schedule posts** — plan your content calendar in advance
- **AI-powered content** — generate captions, optimize posts, and get hashtag suggestions
- **Track performance** — see how your posts are doing with built-in analytics
- **Collaborate** — invite your team with unlimited team members on all plans

## For Developers

Integrate social media publishing into your website, app, or workflow using our SDKs and REST API.

- **One API, 11+ platforms** — publish to Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Telegram, and more with a single call
- **Official SDKs** — PHP, Laravel, and WordPress available now; Node.js, Python, Go, Ruby, and Rust coming soon
- **Cloud-powered** — OwlStack handles all platform APIs, formatting, rate limits, and retries
- **Webhooks & events** — get notified when posts are published or fail
