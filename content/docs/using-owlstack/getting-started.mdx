# Getting Started with OwlStack

Welcome! This guide will walk you through setting up your OwlStack account and publishing your first post to social media. No coding or technical skills required.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [app.owlstack.dev](https://app.owlstack.dev)
2. Sign up with your email or Google account
3. Choose your plan (you can start with the **Starter** plan)

## Step 2: Connect Your Social Accounts

Once you're logged in:

1. Go to **Settings → Social Accounts**
2. Click **Connect** next to the platform you want (e.g., Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn)
3. Follow the authorization steps — OwlStack will ask for permission to post on your behalf
4. That's it! Your account is now connected

You can connect as many accounts as your plan allows. See [Connecting Accounts](./connecting-accounts) for a detailed guide on each platform.

## Step 3: Create Your First Post

1. Click **New Post** from the dashboard
2. Write your message
3. Add images or videos if you want
4. Select which platforms to publish to
5. Click **Publish Now** or **Schedule** for later

OwlStack automatically formats your content for each platform, so you don't have to worry about character limits or image sizes.
