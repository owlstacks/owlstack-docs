# Dashboard Overview

The OwlStack dashboard at [app.owlstack.dev](https://app.owlstack.dev) is your central hub for managing all your social media publishing. Here's what you'll find.

## Home

Your dashboard home shows:

- **Recent posts** — what you've published recently and their status
- **Upcoming scheduled posts** — posts waiting to be published
- **Quick stats** — an overview of your publishing activity
- **Quick actions** — create a new post, view analytics, manage accounts

## Navigation

| Section | What It Does |
|:--------|:-------------|
| **Posts** | Create, edit, and manage your posts |
| **Calendar** | Visual calendar of your scheduled and published posts |
| **Media Library** | Upload and manage images, videos, and other media |
| **Analytics** | See how your posts are performing across platforms |
| **AI Studio** | Use AI to generate and optimize content |
| **Social Accounts** | Connect and manage your social media accounts |
| **Team** | Invite team members and manage permissions |
| **Settings** | Billing, API keys, and account settings |

## Creating Content

From any page, click the **New Post** button to start creating. You can:

- Write your post content with a rich text editor
- Upload and attach media (images, videos, GIFs)
- Select which platforms to publish to
- Preview how your post will look on each platform
- Publish immediately or schedule for later

## Managing Your Accounts

Go to **Social Accounts** to:

- Connect new social media platforms
- See the status of each connected account
- Disconnect accounts you no longer need
- Refresh connections if an authorization has expired
