# Analytics

OwlStack gives you insights into how your posts are performing across all your connected platforms.

## Overview Dashboard

The analytics dashboard shows:

- **Total posts published** across all platforms
- **Engagement metrics** — likes, comments, shares, clicks
- **Platform breakdown** — how each platform is performing
- **Trending content** — your best-performing posts

## Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures |
|:-------|:-----------------|
| **Impressions** | How many times your post was seen |
| **Engagement** | Total interactions (likes, comments, shares) |
| **Clicks** | How many times people clicked your links |
| **Engagement Rate** | Engagement divided by impressions |
| **Reach** | Unique accounts that saw your post |

## Filtering

You can filter analytics by:

- **Date range** — last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or custom
- **Platform** — see performance for a specific platform
- **Post type** — images, videos, text-only

## Reports

Export your analytics data as CSV or PDF to share with your team or clients.

## Platform-Specific Insights

Each platform provides different engagement data. OwlStack normalizes this data so you can compare performance across platforms in a single view.
