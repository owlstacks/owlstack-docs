# Team Collaboration

OwlStack lets you invite team members to collaborate on content creation and publishing.

## Inviting Team Members

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Choose their role
5. Click **Send Invite**

They'll receive an email invitation to join your workspace.

## Roles

| Role | What They Can Do |
|:-----|:----------------|
| **Owner** | Full access, billing, delete workspace |
| **Admin** | Manage team, connect accounts, all post actions |
| **Editor** | Create, edit, schedule, and publish posts |
| **Viewer** | View posts, analytics, and calendar (read-only) |

## Workspaces

Workspaces let you organize different brands, clients, or projects separately. Each workspace has its own:

- Social media accounts
- Posts and drafts
- Analytics
- Team members

| Plan | Workspaces |
|:-----|:-----------|
| **Starter** | 1 |
| **Pro** | 1 |
| **Pro Plus** | 3 |

## Team Features

All plans include **unlimited team members**. You can invite as many people as you need at no extra cost.

### Approval Workflows

Set up approval workflows so posts must be reviewed before publishing:

1. An **Editor** creates a post
2. An **Admin** reviews and approves (or requests changes)
3. Once approved, the post can be published or scheduled
