# Starter Plan

The Starter plan is designed for solo creators, freelancers, and small businesses who want to manage their social media from one place.

## Pricing

- **Monthly**: $19/month
- **Yearly**: $15/month (billed annually)

## What's Included

- **10 social accounts** — connect up to 10 accounts across all supported platforms
- **200 AI credits/month** — use AI to generate captions, hashtags, and optimize content
- **10 GB media library** — store and manage your images and videos
- **1 workspace** — manage one brand or project
- **Basic analytics** — track post performance and engagement
- **Basic API access** — integrate OwlStack into your website or app
- **Unlimited scheduled posts & drafts**
- **Unlimited team members**
- First comment scheduling
- AI Content Studio
- Auto caption generator
- Content calendar
- Viral video library
- Bulk scheduling
- Priority support

## Who Is It For?

- Content creators managing their personal brand
- Freelancers who post regularly to social media
- Small businesses getting started with social media marketing
- Bloggers sharing their content across platforms

## Getting Started

1. [Create an account](https://app.owlstack.dev)
2. Choose the Starter plan
3. Connect your social accounts
4. Start creating and scheduling posts!
