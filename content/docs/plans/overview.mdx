## Choosing a plan

- **Free / Starter**: Perfect for personal projects, blogs, or trying OwlStack out
- **Pro**: For businesses that need scheduling, batch operations, and analytics
- **Pro + AI**: For teams that want AI-powered content optimization
- **Enterprise**: For organizations that need SLAs, dedicated support, or self-hosting
