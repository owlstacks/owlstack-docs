# Pro Plan

The Pro plan is for businesses and teams that need advanced publishing features.

## Everything in Starter, plus

- **5,000 posts per month**
- **Batch publishing** — publish to multiple platforms in a single API call with parallel execution
- **Scheduling** — schedule posts for future publishing with cron expressions or specific dates
- **Delivery logging** — detailed logs of every publish attempt, retry, and result
- **Templates** — reusable post templates with variable substitution
- **Analytics** — track engagement, clicks, and reach across platforms

## Use cases

- Marketing teams managing multiple social accounts
- SaaS products with social media integrations
- Content agencies publishing on behalf of clients
- News sites automating social distribution
